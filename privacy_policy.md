# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2025-11-06
**Last Updated:** 2025-11-06

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how YAWN chrome extension ("we," "our," or "the Extension") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension and associated services. By using the Extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 When You Are Logged In
When you choose to log in to our server, we collect and store:
- **Email address** - Used for user identification
- **Google ID** - Used for authentication and authorization
- **Note content** - The text of sticky notes you create
- **Website information** - URLs and page information where notes are placed
- **Timestamps** - When notes are created, modified, or deleted

### 2.2 When You Are Not Logged In
When you use the Extension without logging in:
- **No data is collected or transmitted to our servers**
- All note data remains locally on your device
- No tracking or analytics are performed

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:
- **Authentication and Authorization:** To verify your identity and provide access to your saved notes
- **Service Functionality:** To save, retrieve, and synchronize your notes across devices when logged in
- **AI Integration:** To process your notes with AI services when you explicitly request this feature using predefined prompts
- **Technical Support:** For debugging and maintenance purposes to ensure proper service operation

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage
- All user data is stored on our secure servers
- Data is currently stored in an unencrypted format
- Only authenticated users can access their own note data through the Extension

### 4.2 Administrative Access
- System administrators may access user data for:
  - Debugging technical issues
  - Performing necessary maintenance
  - Ensuring service reliability
- Administrative access is limited to essential personnel only

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for any purpose.**

Your data may only be disclosed in the following circumstances:
- **With your explicit consent:** If you specifically authorize us to share information
- **Legal requirements:** If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
- **Protection of rights:** To protect the rights, property, or safety of our service, users, or the public

## 6. AI Processing

When you choose to send notes to AI services:
- This action is initiated only at your explicit request
- Notes and website information are processed using predefined prompts
- AI processing is optional and not required for basic Extension functionality

## 7. Your Rights and Controls

### 7.1 Data Access
You can view all your stored notes directly through the Extension when logged in.

### 7.2 Data Deletion
You have the right to delete all data associated with your user account at any time:
- Use the deletion feature within the Extension
- This will permanently remove all your notes and associated website information from our servers
- Deletion is immediate and irreversible

### 7.3 Account Deactivation
You can stop using the service at any time by:
- Logging out of the Extension
- Uninstalling the Extension from Chrome
- Requesting complete data deletion

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our Extension is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

## 9. Local Storage

When not logged in, the Extension may use Chrome's local storage to maintain your notes on your device. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to our servers
- Can be cleared through Chrome's settings

## 10. Third-Party Services

### 10.1 Google Authentication
We use Google OAuth for authentication purposes. Google's use of your information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

### 10.2 AI Services
When you choose to use AI features, your data may be processed by third-party AI services according to their respective privacy policies.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised.

## 12. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as:
- Your account remains active
- You continue to use the Extension while logged in
- Until you request deletion of your data

## 15. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:
- The right to know what personal information we collect
- The right to delete your personal information
- The right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)
- The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

## 16. European Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:
- The right to access your personal data
- The right to rectification of inaccurate data
- The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- The right to data portability
- The right to object to processing
